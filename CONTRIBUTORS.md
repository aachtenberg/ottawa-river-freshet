# Contributors

This project is built on the work of community members in the Northern
Reservoirs Flood Watch group, the broader Ottawa River basin community,
and the maintainer.

## Attribution policy

Specific community members whose research and field expertise underpin
the case-file analysis are referenced throughout the repo by **placeholder
labels** (`Contributor A`, `Contributor B`, etc.) rather than by name.

The reason: this repo is publicly archived and indexed. Naming a
community member ties them to a public position they may not have
explicitly endorsed for that medium. Several contributors have shared
information generously in a community context (Facebook group threads,
direct conversations, working documents) without expectation that their
names would be cross-referenced into a permanent public record alongside
specific analytical claims.

**The placeholders are not pseudonyms.** They map to real people whose
work is acknowledged here. The maintainer holds the mapping privately and
will replace placeholders with real names as individual contributors
explicitly consent to attribution.

If you are a contributor referenced in this repo and would like to be
named (or to remain pseudonymous, or to have specific claims attributed
or removed), please contact the maintainer.

  Cited in Exhibits A, B, C, and the analysis summary.
